Kidjo isn't working properly

If Kidjo isn't working properly:

Step 1 – Restart the app:
Fully close and then restart the app. This often does the trick, since our devices can get a bit overwhelmed sometimes.

Step 2 – Update the app:
Sometimes we find and fix a bug. Keep the app updated for these fixes.

Step 3 – Reinstall the app:
Sometimes crashes happen because of the specific combination of device, platform, OS and programs. Reinstalling the app – deleting it and re-downloading it – can solve a lot of issues.

Step 4 - contact us:
If you're still having problems with the app, then it's time to contact us. Our personalized support will get to the bottom of the issue.

Please email support@kidjo.tv with a description of the problem and the following info: 1. The device you're using 2. Your OS version (if applicable) 3. What connection you're using

We'll do everything we can to get your Kidjo account up and running again!
