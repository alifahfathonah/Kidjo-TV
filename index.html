Kidjo is a digital playground that empowers kids to make their own choices.
They choose what they like, what they play and what they learn.

What is Kidjo and What we believe in

Kidjo is made for kids Period.

We believe in exciting stories, big laughs and smart ideas.

We believe in helping kids on their path to learning the skills and values they need.

We believe our kids' screens should represent the diversity all around us. Everyone should feel invited and included.

We believe in user experience for young minds and hearts and eyes and those sticky little fingers, too.

We believe in finding out what kids think - If they don't love it, we don't keep it!

We believe in giving parents the safety of parental controls, individual user profiles & certified content.

We believe in subscription without ads or any other unwelcome surprises.

Who we are

Kidjo is a team of developers and snack-makers, educators and silly-dancers, designers and nappy-changers – we are experts parents ...and kids at heart!

Kidjo combines our love for good stories, our passion for great user experience and the joy of seeing the sparkle in kids' eyes.

“Born with TV, I grew up with the Internet... When my kid was old enough to watch nursery rhymes or cartoons, I tried to find an app I felt good about, but I only found kids' apps with poor content OR adult video apps with kids' content. I wouldn’t give my adult tennis racket to my 6-year old kid, but rather get him a racket that is suitable for his age, so why would I let my kid use an app conceived for adults? So, I created Kidjo – an app with content kids love (I watched them all and love them, too ^^) and can use by themselves without external help. Today, more than 500 000 parents love us and that's our greatest reward!”

Martin Poix
CEO / Founder

Learning

Kids are like sponges that soak up every little thing they hear and see. Research has proven that the experiences kids make in early childhood are extremely important for their physical and mental development.

That's why every little thing on Kidjo was created with a specific set of skills and values in mind to make sure kids have the most empowering experience when they play and learn.

Our Products

How kids experience the world is at the heart of Kidjo. We want kids to feel free to discover everything our digital playground has to offer. Kidjo gives kids a sense of independence and empowerment as they explore, learn and play

Kidjo TV

When we had our first kid and began introducing screen-time, we looked for something we could really feel good about. But nothing seemed like a safe, age-appropriate and fun environment made especially for kids. So we decided to create it ourselves!

After many months of hard working, wireframing, developing, testing and viewing thousands of hours of content, we finally launched Kidjo TV in 2016.
